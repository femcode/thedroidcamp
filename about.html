About Us

Our Misson

We are determined to introduce newbies and oldies interested in Android development to the framework.

Our Vision

We hope to build a budding community of world class android developers who can take on real world projects

Our Goal

One step at a time, we seek to mentor, train and guide budding android developers on their journey, and have fun while at it. .

A Little Peep

After the initial registration and test, we admit all successful candidates to our workspace, Droidcamp is fully remote so anyone from any part of the world can apply, we assign mentors and team up while admitting. We are bent on maintaining a personal relationship between mentors and mentees, so, we communicate, talk and play games every other day..

camp currently runs for 5 weeks, every day, we hold lessons and give mini tasks so you can see what you learned come to life, we have games and bigger tasks during weekends. We also have a leaderboard for team of the week" and "Mentee of the week". This title is earned by the best performing team and mentee respectively, every week.

We also have a blog and active pages on social media where we share updates from the camp and post camp related articles.

This is just a tip of the iceberg, there's a lot more you stand to gain with droidcamp, join us!
